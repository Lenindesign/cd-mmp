# Storyboard

## 0:00-0:04 - Opening

Dark C/D grid background. Logo and title card introduce the walkthrough. Browser frame enters on the right with the budget step already loaded.

## 0:04-0:10 - Step 1: Budget

Cursor clicks the vehicle-price starting card, adjusts the target slider, then clicks Continue. Caption explains that the shopper can start from price or payment.

## 0:10-0:16 - Step 2: Loan Terms

Loan page fades in. Cursor taps the down-payment field, APR slider, 60-month term, then Continue. Caption focuses on the levers that change cost.

## 0:16-0:22 - Step 3: Vehicle

Vehicle page fades in. Cursor chooses a vehicle path, clicks the search field, highlights the selected trim area, then continues. Caption connects vehicle choice to budget fit.

## 0:22-0:28 - Step 4: Trade And Fees

Trade page fades in. Cursor clicks trade value, state/tax areas, and final estimate. Caption explains purchase context.

## 0:28-0:36 - Step 5: Review

Review page fades in and scrolls through the estimate. Cursor expands interest detail and clicks email/save area. Caption calls out payment, interest, total paid, and marketplace bridge.

## 0:36-0:40 - End Card

Logo and CTA hold. The final frame remains visible for export-friendly ending.
