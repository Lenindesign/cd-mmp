# Script

## Voiceover / On-Screen Script

Build the estimate before the showroom.

Start with a vehicle price target or the monthly payment you want to stay near.

Now tune the terms. Down payment, APR, and loan length change both the payment and the true cost.

Pick a vehicle, trim, or body style. The calculator keeps the estimate connected to what you are shopping for.

Add trade-in value, taxes, and fees so the numbers reflect the real purchase context.

On review, every assumption rolls into one clear estimate: monthly payment, interest, total paid, and vehicles that fit the budget.

Save it, email it, and shop with the numbers in hand.

## Caption Beats

1. Set the starting budget.
2. Tune the loan terms.
3. Choose the vehicle context.
4. Add trade, taxes, and fees.
5. Review the full estimate.
6. Save and shop with confidence.
