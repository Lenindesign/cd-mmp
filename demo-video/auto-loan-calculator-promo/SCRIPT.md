Your monthly budget should explain more than a payment.

Start with a target price or a comfortable monthly number.

Then refine the terms that change the real cost: down payment, APR, term, taxes, fees, and trade-in.

Car and Driver turns those inputs into a clear estimate, a full cost breakdown, and vehicles that fit your budget.
